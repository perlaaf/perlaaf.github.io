+WELCOME+

Greetings! Welcome to our gaming community blog - Casual Gaming News. We keep it simple and lighthearted while casually enjoying our gaming hobby together. Respecting each other's interests with a fun debate is our top goal! We also want to foster a space where you can enjoy your gaming hobby, share geeky interests and make new friends!

+WHAT DO WE DO?+

Join a community of like minded people who write gaming blogs, review and post about games on a weekly basis. It is strongly encouraged to comment on each other's posts and post at least one monthly review to stay active. There is also a discord community where we help with writing and brainstorming ideas and discuss games. If you become one of our Top Bloggers of the month, you'll be featured at the bottom of our home page!

+WHAT CAN YOU DO?+

There are multiple ways to get involved! You can join our writing team and submit articles to the site. Please see the WHO WE ARE page for information on how to get your writing started link at the top center of this page. You can also join our discord and talk to other gamers and discuss things casually (very essence of why we started!). Please browse each page of our site to learn how to contact us and request to join via the CONTACT page and get the discord invite link. Like, share and follow our social media links too!
