ABOUT US:

We are a group of casual gamers interested in building a community blog, sharing news & reviews on our favorite games. The difference between us and other sites is that our number one priority is to accept everyone's level of commitment to gaming no matter how much or how little your life is about gaming. We don't discriminate based on what your level of "casual" is. Pick up a game every few months or weeks that is up to you.

We also welcome more "hardcore" gamers that play on a daily basis. Whatever gamer "type" you are you can contribute by writing a news or review blog on the site. You can also just participate by reading and commenting on our blogs. If you are interested in joining the writing team check out the CONTACT US page linked at the top right corner.

If you're seeking a more interactive community follow our twitch channel!
